_____Three of the Most Famous Paintings in the World____

Painting, the expression of ideas and emotions, with the creation of certain aesthetic qualities,
in a two-dimensional visual language.
The elements of this language—its shapes, lines, colours, tones, and textures—are used in various
ways to produce sensations of volume, space, movement, and light on a flat surface.

Las Meninas
  • Artist: Diego Velázquez

  • Painting style: Baroque

  • Year created: 1656

  • Location: Prado Museum, Madrid, Spain

Guernica
  • Artist: Pablo Picasso

  • Painting style: Cubism

  • Year created: 1937

  • Location: Museum of Modern Art, New York, USA

Mona Lisa
  • Artist: Leonardo da Vinci

  • Painting style: Renaissance

  • Year created: 1503

  • Location: Louvre Museum, Paris, France
